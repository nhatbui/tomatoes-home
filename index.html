Viewer Interaction!

Twitch Tomatoes is a Twitch overlay extension that allows viewers to throw tomatoes at your stream!

Design Your Own Throwable!

We're aiming to be the best extension for viewer interaction for your stream. However, nobody watches a stream because of an extension. They watch it for YOU!

So we want to make something special, unique to you, available to your audience. That's why we're inviting you to design a throwable!

Think of a food and get creative with it! A melon with your signature wig? A pineapple with cat ears?

Reach out to us on Discord and we'll make it happen!
